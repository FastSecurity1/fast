User Experience Program Statement

In order to enhance user experience of our product and service, Fast Security team  develop the User Experience Program (hereinafter “Program”). 

Program aims to collect and analyze some usage data of our product and service, and then improve the user experience. Program is free for user, and participation in Program will not interrupt or interfere with your mobile activities, such as by imposing pop-up dialog boxes, or requiring mail or telephone surveys, etc. 

Your participation in Program is completely voluntary. You can choose to take part in or not before you launch Fast Security, and you are free to quit the Program at any time without any reason. Taking part or not will not affect your access to Fast Security. We recognize that user’s privacy is important, so we have created this Statement to explain the Program. 

By agreeing to take part in Program means you CONSENT to us as follows. 

The Program collects the following types of information: 

1. Feature usage statistics during AB Test: 

Statistics during AB Test period about how you use our app, including what the actions you take when using the app (such as buttons you click on in the app), how frequently you use a certain feature, to what extent a feature is being used, and so on. 

2. Basic application experience statistics: 

Statistics about how the app behaves in consider of its performance, such as how long the junk is scanned, how much the junk is scanned and what is being scanned; 

How your information is used: 

Information collected pursuant to Program will be used only for the purpose of analyzing, evaluating, reviewing and improving the user experience of our product and service. We will not share your information with any third party and will not use your information for other commercial purposes, unless we obtain your consent. 

The security of the information: 

We use reasonable electronic, personnel, and physical measures to protect information from loss, theft, alteration, or misuse. However, please be advised that even the best security measures cannot fully eliminate all risks. We cannot guarantee that only authorized persons will view your information. We are not responsible for third party circumvention of any privacy settings or security measures.
